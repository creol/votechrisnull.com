What I Want to Accomplish

My priorities if elected to County Council

Reduce Taxes

I will lower taxes through responsible spending and by consolidating duplicate services under city control to eliminate double taxation.

Public Safety

I will strengthen cooperation among all levels of law enforcement to keep our county safe. Including support for cooperating with U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement to ensure safe operations.

Shrink Government

As cities grow, the county should shrink. SLCo is the county's fourth-largest employer; we must reduce bureaucracy and empower cities to lead, bringing government closer to the people.

Meet Chris Null

Experience. Leadership. Results.

A Proven Leader for Salt Lake County

Chris Null is a dedicated conservative who believes that government works best when it's closest to the people. As a longtime advocate for local control and responsible governance, Chris has been on the front lines fighting against top-down mandates that threaten our Utah way of life.

Chris is a passionate defender of election integrity and has worked with organizations like Keep My Voice to ensure that Utah voters have a real say in choosing their representatives. He believes that the caucus-convention system gives grassroots the power to hold politicians accountable.

Chris’ core beliefs begin with keeping housing affordable, protecting taxpayers through lower taxes and responsible county spending, and limiting the size and reach of government. He supports parental rights in education, public safety and the rule of law, free enterprise and small businesses, and the protection of individual liberties—including the sanctity of life and Second Amendment rights—while advocating for personal responsibility, economic opportunity, secure borders, and a strong national defense.
